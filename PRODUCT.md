# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Wedding guests, family members, sponsors, and the couple's planning team use the site as a digital invitation, schedule reference, photo keepsake, and RSVP path. Visitors are likely arriving from a shared link on mobile first, with family members also reviewing entourage and sponsor names carefully on larger screens.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Amelia and Theo's wedding as a cinematic garden invitation while keeping practical guest tasks close at hand: learn the story, confirm the schedule, understand the dress code, browse photos, and submit an RSVP. Success means the page feels personal and keepsake-worthy without making the RSVP flow feel slow or confusing.

## Brand Personality

Heirloom, tender, composed. The voice is warm and grateful, with a soft floral sensibility and enough structure to feel premium rather than ornamental.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered invitation templates, harsh black overlays, generic icon-card grids, repeated tiny uppercase section labels, and overly beige pages where all sections feel identical. Avoid mixing illustration styles or using sketchy/doodle floral art.

## Design Principles

- Let the couple's photography carry the emotional weight.
- Use botanical ornament as a signature, not as filler.
- Keep family lists legible and formal.
- Make RSVP feel graceful but unmistakably usable.
- Preserve a light, romantic mood while meeting accessibility basics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible semantic HTML, labeled form controls, keyboard-friendly navigation and lightbox controls, visible focus states, readable contrast, reduced-motion handling, and alt text for meaningful photography.
